Privacy Policy

Snaprix — AI Memory Assistant
Last updated: May 7, 2026

At Snaprix, your privacy is our core foundation. We believe that your digital memories should remain private, secure, and under your absolute control. This policy outlines how we handle your data with the highest security standards.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: To provide our services, we collect your email address. If you use Google Sign-In, we receive your name and profile picture URL from Google's authentication service.

Your Content (Memories): Screenshots, images, voice notes, and text descriptions you upload are stored securely in your private vault. We do not access this content unless required for technical processing you initiate.

Technical Data: We collect basic device information (model, OS version) and anonymized usage analytics to maintain app stability and improve performance.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To manage your account and synchronize memories across your devices.
  • To perform AI-powered analysis and organization on the content you submit.
  • To provide customer support and troubleshoot technical issues.
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our platform.

3. AI Processing & Third Parties

When AI features are active, specific text or image content is processed via OpenAI's API. This data is transmitted through encrypted channels and is used solely to provide the analysis you requested. OpenAI does not use this data to train their models for API users.

4. Data Security & Infrastructure

We utilize Supabase (built on PostgreSQL) for our backend infrastructure. Your data is protected with:

  • 256-bit AES encryption at rest.
  • TLS 1.3 encryption for all data in transit.
  • Row-Level Security (RLS), ensuring that only you can access your specific data records.

5. Your Rights & Data Deletion

Account and Data Deletion

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time. We provide two ways to do this:

  • In-App: Navigate to Profile Settings > Delete Account. This action is immediate and permanent.
  • By Request: Contact us at snaprix.support@gmail.com from your registered email address. We will process your deletion request within 48 hours.

Upon deletion, all your memories, account details, and stored files are permanently purged from our servers.
